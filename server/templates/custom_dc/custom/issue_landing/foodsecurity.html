Data Commons has statistics from the CDC about the prevalence of various health conditions that may be of interest to food security organizations. Some examples are:

  • Diabetes
  • Coronary Heart Disease
  • High Cholesterol
  • High Blood Pressure
These statistics are not broken down by poverty status, but they can be combined with other data about poverty in Data Commons, such as:
  • Number of Family Households Below the Poverty Level in the Past 12 Months
  • Number of People Who Are Below Poverty Level Status in the Past 12 Months
